Welcome to YESGrant! Our mission is to mentor aspiring undergraduate, graduate, and PhD students to secure fully funded scholarships and zero-tuition admissions at top public universities worldwide. We provide personalized mentorship from experts who have walked the same path, having earned fully-funded scholarships.

What is fully funded scholarship?

The students scholarship to pursue master or any future graduate studies varies depending on the country, and as well the scholarship that the student chooses to apply for. To give an example, if you are interested to apply for a master's in Europe and especially in France, on an average the scholarship will be provided for the whole two years of the master's where the selected student will receive an amount between 10,000 - 14,017€ per year and apart from that a maximum of 1,000€ for travel and visa expenses.
