Your Path to Zero Tuition-Find the Right Program for You.

Whether you need basic guidance or full support, we have programs tailored to fit your needs. Each is designed to help you build your application with our expertise and tools.

Self-Study Program

Access our comprehensive online course focused on the five pillars of admission: building value for grad studies, crafting a resume, writing motivation and cover letters, and application strategy. By completing the course and task modules, you'll have a first draft of your application materials ready.

Mentorship Program

Includes the self-study course and once, one-on-one session with an expert. You gain an interaction with a mentor to review your application materials and suggest improvements.

Full Support Program

Includes the self-study course and full mentorship from start to finish, personalized feedback, and online accessible templates for all application materials, categorized based on different university requirements, and scholarships.
